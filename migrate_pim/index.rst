Upgrade Akeneo PIM projects
===========================

This chapter describes how to apply a patch on your current version or how to upgrade from a major version to a more recent one.

As a prerequisite, we assume that you are using a project installed from a Community Edition or an Enterprise Edition (:doc:`/install_pim/index`).
